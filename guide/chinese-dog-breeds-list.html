Rare Chinese Dog Breeds You Can Import

A complete list of rare Chinese dog breeds you can import worldwide — Chow Chow, Shih Tzu, Chinese Crested, Pekingese, Shar-Pei and Chongqing Dog — and who…

China has some of the world's oldest and rarest dog breeds. Here are the breeds we export, with who each one suits best.

The breeds

  • Chow Chow — lion-maned icon, top seller in the Middle East
  • Shih Tzu — gentle apartment companion, popular in Korea and Japan
  • Chinese Crested — rare, low-shedding, cabin-friendly
  • Pekingese — imperial lapdog with deep history in Japan
  • Shar-Pei — wrinkled, loyal guardian
  • Chongqing Dog — ultra-rare collector breed

Which breed is right for you?

Apartment in Korea or Japan? Choose a small breed. Want a statement dog in the Middle East? Chow Chow or Shar-Pei. Collector seeking rarity? The Chongqing Dog.
