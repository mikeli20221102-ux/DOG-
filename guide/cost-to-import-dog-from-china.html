How Much Does It Cost to Import a Dog from China? (2026 Breakdown)

Full cost breakdown for importing a puppy from China: breed price, microchip, rabies titer test, permits and escort delivery. Real 2026 numbers by breed and…

The total cost of importing a dog from China has two parts: the breed price and the delivery + compliance cost. Knowing both up front prevents surprises.

1. Breed price (from China)

BreedFrom (USD)
Shih Tzu / Shar-Pei$900
Pekingese$1,000
Chinese Crested$1,200
Chow Chow$1,500
Chongqing Dog$1,800

2. Compliance costs

  • ISO microchip — included in our preparation
  • Rabies vaccination + antibody titer test — typically $80–$200
  • Health certificate and export permit — varies by destination country

3. Delivery (escort or cargo)

International flight-nanny or air-cargo delivery is quoted by destination city and breed size — usually the largest single line item. Small breeds to Korea, Japan or Singapore cost less to move than large breeds to the Middle East.
