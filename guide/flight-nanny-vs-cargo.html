Flight Nanny vs Air Cargo

Compare flight-nanny (in-cabin escort) and manifest air-cargo delivery for importing a dog from China. Which is best by breed size, route and budget.

Your puppy can travel two main ways from China to your city. We help you choose based on breed size, route and budget.

Flight nanny (in-cabin escort)

  • A professional pet escort flies with your puppy, often in-cabin for small breeds
  • Lowest stress — the dog is cared for the whole journey
  • Ideal for Chinese Crested, Shih Tzu and other small breeds

Manifest air cargo (escorted)

  • Temperature-controlled, IATA-compliant travel crate in the hold
  • Required for larger breeds such as Chow Chow
  • Still coordinated and accompanied by our team end-to-end

What is always included

Airline booking, IATA crate, export and destination paperwork, plus live updates with chain-of-custody photos. You pay the balance on safe arrival.
