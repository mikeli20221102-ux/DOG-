Best Apartment-Friendly Chinese Dog Breeds (Korea & Japan)

The best small, low-shedding Chinese dog breeds for apartment living in Korea, Japan and Singapore — Chinese Crested, Shih Tzu and Pekingese compared.

City living in Korea, Japan or Singapore calls for small, quiet, low-shedding dogs. These Chinese breeds are the best apartment companions.

Top apartment breeds

  • Chinese Crested — 4–5 kg, low shedding, often cabin-eligible
  • Shih Tzu — gentle, sociable, 4–7 kg
  • Pekingese — calm imperial lapdog, 3–6 kg

What makes a good apartment dog

  • Small size and moderate energy
  • Low noise and low shedding
  • Adaptable, people-focused temperament

Importing to your city

All three travel well by flight nanny. See the Korea and Japan import guides.
