How to Import a Dog from China to the Philippines (2026 Guide)

Step-by-step guide to importing a puppy from China to the Philippines: BAI import permit, microchip, rabies vaccination, timeline and best breeds.

The Philippines is a strong market for Chinese breeds — Chow Chow for premium buyers and Shih Tzu for volume, both very popular on Facebook.

Requirements (Bureau of Animal Industry)

  1. ISO microchip
  2. Rabies vaccination
  3. Veterinary health certificate from China
  4. Import permit from the Bureau of Animal Industry (BAI)
  5. Import clearance on arrival

Timeline

The Philippines is one of our faster markets — typically 1–2 months when paperwork is started promptly.

Best breeds for the Philippines

Chow Chow leads premium demand; Shih Tzu and Pekingese sell in volume. See the Philippines market page.

Insist on a live video call and a signed contract — read how to avoid scams.
