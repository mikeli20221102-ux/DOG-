Importing Chinese Dog Breeds to Russia

How to import Chow Chow, Shar-Pei and rare Chinese breeds to Russia: microchip, rabies vaccination, health certificate, Rosselkhoznadzor rules and timeline.

Russia is a strong market for distinctive Chinese breeds — Chow Chow, Shar-Pei and the rare Chongqing Dog are especially sought after.

Requirements

  1. ISO microchip
  2. Rabies vaccination (valid, given after the microchip)
  3. Veterinary health certificate
  4. Import documentation per Rosselkhoznadzor rules

Timeline

Russia is often faster than titer-required markets — typically 1–2 months when started promptly. We confirm current rules before you reserve.

Best breeds for Russia

Cold-tolerant, statement breeds do best: the Chow Chow's thick coat and the Chongqing Dog's rarity are major draws.
