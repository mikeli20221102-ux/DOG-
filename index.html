FAQ

Good questions, honest answers

How is payment handled?

We do not accept online card payments. After your inquiry and a live video viewing, we arrange a deposit by bank transfer with a signed sales contract. The balance is paid on the puppy’s safe arrival.

Which countries can you deliver to?

It depends on your country’s live-animal import rules. We currently focus on markets with workable requirements such as South Korea, Southeast Asia and the Middle East. Some countries restrict or ban commercial dog imports — we’ll confirm your country before you reserve.

How long does it take?

Because of compliance steps (microchip, rabies vaccination, antibody titer test, health certificate and import permits), delivery typically takes 2–4 months, sometimes longer for stricter destinations.

Are the puppies healthy?

Every puppy is vet-checked, vaccinated and microchipped, and comes with pedigree and health documentation plus a health guarantee written into the contract.

How does delivery work?

A professional pet escort / flight nanny accompanies your puppy in-person and hand-delivers them to you, coordinating airline, crate and customs paperwork.
